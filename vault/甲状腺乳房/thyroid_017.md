## Overview
Total bilateral thyroidectomy with thyroid fossa excision for bilateral thyroid nodules and isthmus adenoma.

## Diagnosis
**Pre-operative:** Bilateral thyroid nodules
**Intraoperative:** Bilateral thyroid + isthmus adenoma
**Pathological:** Frozen section (F08-3541): "Left and right small nodules + isthmus: adenoma."

## Intraoperative Findings
Bilateral thyroid nodules measuring approximately 1.5 cm in diameter, soft in consistency, with clear borders. Isthmus adenoma measuring approximately 3 cm, cystic and soft in consistency. Intraoperative frozen pathology as above.

## Operative Steps

1. Supine position, under general anesthesia, routine disinfection and draping.
2. Arc-shaped incision 2 cm above the sternal notch, sequentially opening the skin, subcutaneous tissue, and platysma.游离皮瓣上至甲状软骨，下至胸骨上凹；
3. Open the superficial cervical fascia along the white line of the neck, open the perithyroidic fascia, and explore as found during surgery;
4. Free the middle pole of the left thyroid gland, transect the left thyroid middle vessels, and thoroughly mobilize the lateral part of the left thyroid gland with loose adhesions. Transect the left thyroid inferior vessels near the inferior pole. Use hemostats to determine the resection range of the left thyroid gland, paying attention to the course of the left recurrent laryngeal nerve, and resect most of the left thyroid gland and thyroid isthmus within the capsule, retaining the superior pole as the thyroid gland, and suture the remaining capsule;
5. Similarly, resect the middle pole of the right thyroid gland and isthmus together;
6. Thoroughly hemostasis, clean the wound, and leave a 16F T-tube in the right thyroid fossa. Suture the anterior neck muscles, and close the incision in layers;
7. Operation completed.
