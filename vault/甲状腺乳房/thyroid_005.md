## Overview
Mastectomy is a surgical procedure for the removal of breast tissue, typically performed for breast cancer. It involves the removal of the entire breast, including the nipple, areola, and surrounding tissue.

## Diagnosis
**Pre-operative:** Right breast cancer
**Intraoperative:** Right breast cancer
**Pathological:** Invasive ductal carcinoma of the right breast

## Intraoperative Findings
Right breast mass excised in the lower outer quadrant.

## Operative Steps
1. General anesthesia, routine disinfection and draping.
2.梭形切口以乳房为中心，上端自锁骨中外1/3交界处下方，下端达肋弓。剥离皮瓣，上到锁骨，下达肋弓，外侧至背阔肌前缘，内侧为胸骨旁。
3.剥离乳房、周围脂肪连同胸大肌筋膜至胸小肌。
4.整块移除标本，冲洗，置入负压引流三根于胸骨旁、腋窝，戳创引出。
5.完善止血，清点器械纱布无误，逐层关闭切口, 术毕。

## Anatomical Key Points
> Critical structures include the breast, pectoralis major muscle, pectoralis minor muscle, and axillary lymph nodes. (乳房，胸大肌，胸小肌，腋窝淋巴结)

## Teaching Points
- Understanding the anatomy of the breast and surrounding structures is crucial for successful mastectomy.
- Proper dissection and identification of key structures are essential to minimize complications.

## Complication Prevention
- Careful hemostasis and thorough surgical technique are key to preventing complications.
