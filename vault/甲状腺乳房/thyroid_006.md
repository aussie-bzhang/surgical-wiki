## Overview
A modified radical mastectomy for breast cancer, involving the removal of the breast, surrounding tissue, and axillary lymph nodes, with a focus on preserving the pectoralis major muscle.

## Diagnosis
**Pre-operative:** Left breast mass
**Intraoperative:** Left breast cancer
**Pathological:** Invasive ductal carcinoma of the left breast

## Intraoperative Findings
A 3cm diameter mass on the lateral aspect of the left breast with unclear borders, approximately 4cm from the nipple. Multiple enlarged lymph nodes in the left axilla.

## Operative Steps
1. Place the patient in a supine position, perform routine disinfection and draping, local anesthesia, and make a radiating incision over the mass. Incise the skin, subcutaneous tissue, and breast tissue, and excise the mass for frozen section pathology.
2. Administer general anesthesia, perform routine disinfection and draping.
3. Make a transverse incision around the breast, extending laterally to the axillary前线 and medially to the sternal border.
4.剥离皮瓣，上至锁骨，下达肋弓，外侧至背阔肌前缘，内侧为胸骨旁。
5. Dissect the breast, surrounding fat, and pectoralis major fascia to the pectoralis minor muscle, and clear the intermuscular lymph nodes.
6. Open the axilla, expose the subclavian vessels, axillary vessels, and brachial plexus, and clear the fat and lymphatic tissue in front and below the axillary vein, and the fat and lymphatic tissue around the subclavian vessels.
7. Remove the specimen en bloc, irrigate, and place two negative pressure drains in the chest wall and axilla, and bring them out through the stab wounds.
8. Perfect hemostasis, count instruments and sponges, and close the incision in layers. The operation is completed.

## Anatomical Key Points
> Critical structures include the breast, axillary lymph nodes, pectoralis major muscle, and submammary gland. (乳房, 腋窝淋巴结, 胸大肌, 乳腺)
