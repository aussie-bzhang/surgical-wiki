## Overview
Total thyroidectomy and partial thyroidectomy for bilateral thyroid adenoma, with emphasis on the dissection and preservation of critical structures such as the trachea and jugular veins.

## Diagnosis
**Pre-operative:** Bilateral thyroid adenoma
**Intraoperative:** Bilateral thyroid adenoma
**Pathological:** Cystic degeneration of thyroid adenoma

## Intraoperative Findings
A 1.5cm mass in the middle-lower pole of the left thyroid lobe, soft in consistency, and a 0.8cm mass near the isthmus of the right thyroid lobe, soft in consistency. Intraoperative frozen pathology confirmed the findings.

## Operative Steps
1. Supine position, under general anesthesia, routine disinfection and draping.
2. Arcuate incision 2 cm above the sternal notch, sequentially opening the skin, subcutaneous tissue, and platysma, freeing the flap up to the thyroid cartilage and down to the sternal notch.
3. Open the superficial cervical muscles along the white line of the neck, open the outer capsule of the thyroid, and explore as found during surgery.
4. Free the middle pole of the left thyroid, transect the middle thyroid vessels, and thoroughly free the lateral part of the thyroid from loose adhesions. Transect the isthmus and ligate for hemostasis. Near the superior pole, transect and ligate the superior pole vessels. Use hemostats to determine the resection range of the thyroid, paying attention to the course of the recurrent laryngeal nerve, and resect most of the left thyroid gland in front of the thyroid capsule.
5. Free the lateral part of the right thyroid from loose adhesions, use hemostats to determine the resection range of the right thyroid mass, resect the mass, and suture the capsule.
6. Thorough hemostasis, clean the wound, leave a T-tube drain, suture the anterior cervical muscles, and close the incision in layers.
7. Operation completed.

## Anatomical Key Points
> Critical structures include the trachea, jugular veins, and recurrent laryngeal nerve. The common bile duct (胆总管) should be protected during dissection.
