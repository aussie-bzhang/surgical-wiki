## Overview
Total thyroidectomy with resection of isthmus and thyroglossal cyst removal for a right thyroid adenoma and thyroglossal cyst.

## Diagnosis
**Pre-operative:** Right thyroid mass
**Intraoperative:** Right thyroid adenoma, thyroglossal cyst
**Pathological:** Atypical adenoma, incomplete capsule

## Intraoperative Findings
Intraoperative findings revealed a 4.0 cm soft mass in the right thyroid with a complete capsule, no abnormalities in the left thyroid, and a 3.0 cm thyroglossal cyst.

## Operative Steps
1. Supine position, under general anesthesia, routine disinfection and draping.
2. Arcuate incision 2 cm above the sternal notch.
3. Dissection of skin, subcutaneous tissue, and platysma, flap elevation to the thyroid cartilage.
4. Incision along the cervical white line, opening the superficial cervical muscles, and exposing the thyroid capsule.
5. Dissection of the right thyroid middle and lower poles, transection of thyroid vessels, and complete dissection of the lateral loose adhesions, transection of the isthmus.
6. Dissection of the right thyroid superior pole, transection of thyroid vessels, and resection of the right thyroid gland with protection of the right recurrent laryngeal nerve.
7. Dissection of the thyroglossal cyst to the hyoid bone, resection of part of the hyoid bone, and resection of the cyst close to the root of the fistula.
8. Complete hemostasis, wound irrigation, placement of a negative pressure drain, and layered closure of the incision.
