## Overview
A modified radical mastectomy for breast cancer, involving the removal of the breast, surrounding tissue, and axillary lymph nodes, with the preservation of the pectoralis major muscle.

## Diagnosis
**Pre-operative:** Right breast mass
**Intraoperative:** Right breast cancer
**Pathological:** Invasive ductal carcinoma of the right breast

## Intraoperative Findings
Right breast mass measuring 2.0 cm in the outer upper quadrant, unclear borders, approximately 2 cm from the nipple. No enlarged lymph nodes in the right axilla.

## Operative Steps
1. Place the patient in a supine position, perform routine disinfection and draping, local anesthesia, and make a radiating incision over the mass. Incise the skin, subcutaneous tissue, and breast tissue, and excise the mass for frozen section.
2. Administer general anesthesia, perform routine disinfection and draping.
3. Make an incision around the breast, with the upper end below the midpoint of the clavicle and the lower end reaching the costal arch.剥离皮瓣，上至锁骨，下达肋弓，外侧至背阔肌前缘，内侧为胸骨旁。
4. Dissect the breast, surrounding fat, and pectoralis major fascia to the pectoralis minor muscle, and clear the intermuscular lymph nodes.
5. Open the axilla, expose the subclavian vessels, axillary vessels, and brachial plexus, and clear the fat and lymphatic tissue in front and below the axillary vein, and the fat and lymphatic tissue around the subclavian vessels.
6. Remove the specimen en bloc, irrigate, and place three negative pressure drains in the chest wall and axilla, and bring them out through the puncture wounds.
7. Perfect hemostasis, count instruments and sponges, and close the incision in layers. The operation is completed.

## Anatomical Key Points
> Critical structures: breast, axillary lymph nodes, pectoralis major muscle, pectoralis minor muscle. (乳腺，腋窝淋巴结，胸大肌，胸小肌)
