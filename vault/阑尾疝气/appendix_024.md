## Overview
Appendectomy for acute suppurative appendicitis following portal hypertension surgery.

## Diagnosis
**Pre-operative:** Portal hypertension post-surgery, acute appendicitis
**Intraoperative:** Portal hypertension post-surgery, acute suppurative appendicitis
**Pathological:** Not documented

## Intraoperative Findings
Intraoperatively, the appendix was located below the cecum, measuring approximately 8cm in length. The appendix was全程充血水肿, with a head diameter of 1.0cm and a body root diameter of 0.5cm. There was approximately 15ml of purulent exudate around the appendix.

## Operative Steps
1. Supine position, under general anesthesia, routine disinfection and draping.
2. Right lower quadrant McBurney's point oblique incision, layer-by-layer opening, aspiration of exudate, exploration as above.
3. Elevate the appendix head, ligate the appendiceal mesentery in segments to the root of the appendix.
4. Cut the appendix 0.5cm from the root, remove the specimen, ligate the root of the appendix, cauterize the stump with an electrosurgical knife, and bury it in a purse-string suture, covering it with an ileocecal lipoma.
5. After thorough hemostasis, count instruments and sponges, and close the abdomen in layers.
6. Operation completed.
