## Overview
Appendectomy for acute suppurative appendicitis, highlighting the technical aspects of identifying and removing the inflamed appendix.

## Diagnosis
**Pre-operative:** Acute appendicitis.
**Intraoperative:** Acute suppurative appendicitis.
**Pathological:** Not documented

## Intraoperative Findings
The appendix was located at the lower part of the cecum, showing significant congestion and swelling. It measured 8cm in length, 1.0cm in diameter at the base, and 0.8cm at the root. The body of the appendix was necrotic and suppurative with local exudate of 30ml. No abnormalities were found during exploration of the ileocecal junction.

## Operative Steps
1. After successful spinal anesthesia, the patient was placed in a supine position and the abdomen was routinely disinfected and draped. A right McBurney's point oblique incision was made to enter the abdomen.
2. Local exudate was aspirated, and the appendix head was lifted. The mesentery of the appendix was ligated and transected in segments up to the root.
3. The appendix was transected 0.5cm from the root, the specimen was removed, and the stump was cauterized with an electric knife. It was ligated again around the root and covered with an ileal lipoma.

## Anatomical Key Points
> The appendix (阑尾) is located at the lower part of the cecum (盲肠). The ileocecal junction (回盲部) is a critical area for exploration. (胆总管)

## Operative Steps

连硬麻成功后平卧位常规消毒铺巾，取右侧麦氏点斜切口逐层入腹，探察如上述。

吸尽局部渗液，提起阑尾头部，分段结扎切断阑尾系膜至阑尾根部，根部缝扎一道。
