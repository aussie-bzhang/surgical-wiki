## Overview
Duodenal bulb perforation repair in a patient with a clinical presentation of gastrointestinal perforation.

## Diagnosis
**Pre-operative:** Gastrointestinal perforation
**Intraoperative:** Duodenal bulb ulcer perforation
**Pathological:** Not documented

## Intraoperative Findings
Intraoperative findings revealed 100ml of purulent fluid in the abdominal cavity, a 1.0cm ulcer on the anterior wall of the duodenal bulb with a central perforation of 0.2cm, and surrounding tissue was contaminated and edematous.

## Operative Steps
1. Supine position, under general anesthesia, routine disinfection and draping.
2. Midline upper abdominal incision, sequentially opened, exploration as above.
3. Suture the ulcer perforation, cover with omentum tissue.
4. Rinse the abdominal cavity with large amounts of normal saline,彻底止血, close in layers.
5. Completion of surgery.
