## Overview
Appendectomy for acute suppurative appendicitis; key technical feature includes identification and resection of the inflamed appendix.

## Diagnosis
**Pre-operative:** Acute appendicitis
**Intraoperative:** Acute suppurative appendicitis
**Pathological:** Not documented

## Intraoperative Findings
Appendix located in the ileocecal region, approximately 8cm long, 1cm in diameter, with marked congestion and edema throughout, covered with purulent exudate on the surface. Peritoneal purulent effusion approximately 30ml.

## Operative Steps
1. Supine position, spinal-epidural anesthesia, routine disinfection and draping. Right lower quadrant McBurney incision, enter peritoneum as described.
2. Elevate the appendix head, ligate the appendiceal mesentery in segments to the base of the appendix.
3. At 0.5cm from the base of the appendix, perform a double ligature and suture followed by transection. Remove the specimen, cauterize the stump with electrocautery, and cover with a small amount of ileal lipoma.
4. Thorough hemostasis, close the abdomen in layers.
5. Procedure completed.

## Anatomical Key Points
> Critical structures include the appendix and ileocecal junction. The base of the appendix requires careful handling to avoid injury to the ileum. (阑尾和回盲部是关键结构。阑尾根部需要小心处理，以避免损伤回肠。)
