## Overview
Gastrectomy with pyloroplasty for gastric mesenchymoma, involving resection of the upper part of the stomach and pyloric reconstruction.

## Diagnosis
**Pre-operative:** Gastric mesenchymoma
**Intraoperative:** Gastric mesenchymoma
**Pathological:** Not documented

## Intraoperative Findings
Intraoperative findings revealed a 4.0cm x 2.5cm mass on the posterior wall of the lesser curvature of the stomach, involving the gastroesophageal junction. The mucosa was intact and smooth with a moderate texture, protruding into the lumen. No明显ly enlarged lymph nodes were palpated around the stomach. Exploration of the liver, pelvis, and abdominal cavity was negative.

## Operative Steps
1. Patient placed in supine position under general anesthesia. Abdomen explored via a midline upper abdominal incision.
2. Mobilization of the greater curvature vessels to the left of the cardia and division of the vagus nerve.
3. Mobilization of the lesser curvature vessels to the right of the cardia and division of the vagus nerve.
4. Mobilization of the posterior wall of the stomach, division of the gastropancreatic fissure and ligation of the posterior gastric vessels, mobilization to the posterior wall of the cardia.
5. 55mm linear stapler used to transect the stomach body, with interrupted full-thickness sutures for reinforcement. The esophagus was transected 3.0cm above the tumor margin, and a tube-shaped stapler anvil was placed for anastomosis.
6. Incision made in the anterior wall of the stomach body for tube-shaped stapler placement for gastroesophageal anastomosis. Anastomosis reinforced with interrupted full-thickness sutures around the anastomosis and closure of the gastric anterior wall opening with omentum.
7. Incision made in the pyloric submucosal layer, and longitudinal sutures placed for pyloroplasty.
8. No bleeding was noted in the abdominal cavity. The abdomen was irrigated with saline until clean, and two negative pressure drainage tubes were left at the anastomosis site.
9. Sutures removed layer by layer after verifying that all sponges and instruments were accounted for. Postoperative care focused on vital signs and drainage.

## Anatomical Key Points
> Critical structures include the stomach, pylorus, and esophagus. The common bile duct (胆总管) and the pancreas should be protected during mobilization.

## Intraoperative Findings
术中见胃贲门小弯侧后壁一大小4.0cm*2.5cm肿块，累及胃贲门食管连接处，粘膜完整光滑，质地中等，向腔内隆起，胃周未及明显肿大淋巴结，探查肝脏、盆腔、腹腔均阴性。

## Operative Steps

平卧位气静麻成功后，常规消毒铺巾，取上腹正中切口进腹探查如上。

游离胃大弯侧血管分离至贲门左侧同时切断迷走神经。

游离胃小弯侧血管至贲门右侧同时切断迷走神经。

分离胃后壁，切断胃胰劈并结扎胃后血管，分离至胃贲门后壁。

55mm侧侧吻合器切断胃体，残端间断全层缝合加固，距离肿瘤上缘3.0cm处切断食管，置入管状吻合器蘑菇头待吻合。

胃体前壁开孔置入管状吻合器行胃-食管吻合，吻合口间断全层缝合加固一周，间断全层缝合胃前壁开口并覆盖大网膜浆膜化。

切开幽门浆肌层，间断纵行缝合行幽门成形术。

明确腹腔内无出血，生理盐水冲洗腹腔至净，吻合口后留置负压引流管两根。

清点纱布器械无误后逐层关腹，术后注意生命体征及引流情况。
