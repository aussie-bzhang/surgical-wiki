## Overview
Distal gastrectomy with Billroth II reconstruction, antecolic, proximal to greater curvature, D2 lymphadenectomy for gastric adenocarcinoma, combined with complex small bowel adhesion release. Key technical feature: anastomosis of the stomach to the jejunum in the antecolic position.

## Diagnosis
**Pre-operative:** Gastric antrum cancer
**Intraoperative:** Gastric antrum cancer
**Pathological:** Not documented

## Intraoperative Findings
Severe adhesions in the upper right abdomen, dense adhesions between the small intestine, antrum, and gallbladder bed, unclear structures. Tumor located on the lesser curvature of the antrum, diameter 4cm, infiltrative ulcerative type, involving the serosal layer, causing incomplete obstruction of the gastric lumen. Scattered enlarged lymph nodes around the stomach, negative exploration of the small intestine, peritoneum, pelvis, and liver.

## Operative Steps
1. Supine position, after general anesthesia, routine disinfection and draping, midline upper abdominal incision, abdominal exploration as described above;
2. Release dense adhesions in the upper right abdomen, mobilize the small intestine and antrum, identify the gallbladder bed, hepatoduodenal ligament, etc. Incise the gastrocolic ligament on the intestinal side, extend to the right to the hepatic flexure of the colon, and to the inferior pole of the spleen on the left; elevate the transverse colon, strip the omentum, anterior leaf of the transverse mesocolon, and pancreatic capsule, clamp and ligate the right gastroepiploic artery and vein at the vascular root;
3. Clamp and cut the gastro splenic ligament along the greater curvature of the stomach, dissect the 4th and 6th groups of lymph nodes, retain 2 branches of the short gastric vessels, and protect the spleen;
4. Dissect the lesser omentum below the liver, dissect the 8th group of lymph nodes; ligate and cut the right gastric vessels at the root, mobilize the antrum and the first and second segments of the duodenum, close the duodenum 3cm from the pylorus with a 60mm clamp, reinforce the distal end with silk suture, and pack it;
5. Flip the stomach, carefully separate the adhesions between the stomach and the pancreas, ligate and cut the gastroepiploic vessels along the lesser curvature of the stomach upwards, while dissecting the 1st, 3rd, 5th groups of lymph nodes, up to the right side of the esophagus; cut the left gastric artery at the root, ligate and suture the residual segment, dissect the 7th and 9th groups of lymph nodes;
6. Clamp the stomach body 7cm above the tumor margin with two Kocher clamps, close the remaining stomach body with a 60mm clamp, place the anvil of a 28.5mm end-to-end anastomosis stapler through a jejunum incision 20cm from the Treitz ligament and fix it with a purse-string suture, insert the long handle into the residual stomach end to anastomose the stomach with the jejunum in the antecolic position, the input end to the lesser curvature, close the stapler with a 60mm clamp, reinforce the anastomosis with 3-0 gastrointestinal suture, and reinforce the anastomotic mouth, place the gastric tube;
7. Thorough hemostasis, wash the abdominal cavity, aspirate the effluent, place two negative pressure balls in front and behind the anastomosis below the liver, check instruments and gauze, close the abdomen in layers. Operation completed.

## Anatomical Key Points

## Technical Challenges
Difficulties encountered in mobilizing the stomach and duodenum due to dense adhesions, careful hemostasis during vessel ligation, and precise anastomosis of the stomach and jejunum.

## Teaching Points
- The importance of careful mobilization and identification of anatomical structures in the presence of dense adhesions.
- The technique of vessel ligation and anastomosis, emphasizing hemostasis and preventing postoperative complications.
- The management of complex small bowel adhesions.

## Complication Prevention
- Thorough hemostasis to prevent postoperative hemorrhage.
- Proper anastomosis technique to prevent leakage and obstruction.
- Postoperative monitoring for signs of infection and anastomotic leakage.

## Intraoperative Findings

## Operative Steps

取平卧位，气静麻后常规消毒铺巾，取上腹正中切口进腹，探查如上；

松解右上腹致密粘连，游离小肠、胃窦，辨清胆囊床、肝十二指肠韧带等结构。断胃结肠韧带之肠侧，向右至结肠肝曲，向左至脾下极；提起横结肠，剥离大网膜、横结肠系膜前叶及胰腺包膜，于血管根部钳夹、切扎胃网膜右动、静脉；

沿胃体大弯钳夹、切断胃脾韧带，清扫第4、6组淋巴结，保留胃短血管2支，注意保护脾脏；

肝下断小网膜，清扫第8组淋巴结；于根部结扎切断胃右血管，游离胃窦部、十二指肠第一、二段，距幽门3cm处以60mm钳闭器关闭切断十二指肠，残端以丝线全层加固一周后再包埋，胃残端以考克钳钳闭；

翻起胃体，仔细分离胃与胰腺之粘连，沿胃小弯侧向上逐把结扎切断胃冠状血管，同时清扫第1、3、5、组淋巴结，向上达食管右侧；于根部断胃左动脉，残段结扎+缝扎，清扫第7、9组淋巴结；
