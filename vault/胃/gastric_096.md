## Overview
Gastric cancer resection (D2) for gastric cancer, involving upper gastrointestinal tract exploration and lymph node dissection.

## Diagnosis
**Pre-operative:** Gastric cancer
**Intraoperative:** Gastric cancer
**Pathological:** Not documented

## Intraoperative Findings
Gastric mass located on the posterior wall of the lesser curvature of the gastric body, diameter about cm, infiltrative ulcerative type. Not penetrating the serosa. No abnormalities found in liver, gallbladder, pancreas, small intestine, and colon.

## Operative Steps
1. Supine position, general anesthesia successful, routine disinfection and draping, midupper abdominal midline incision, layer-by-layer abdominal exploration as above;
2. Elevate transverse colon, separate and cut the gastrocolic ligament, to the right to the hepatic flexure of the colon, to the left to the inferior pole of the spleen, cut the splenocolic ligament, strip the anterior leaf of the transverse mesocolon to the superior margin of the pancreas, expose the posterior wall of the stomach, cut the gastropancreatic fold.
3. Retain the right gastric网膜血管, and cut the greater omentum at the avascular area of the gastric body, along the greater curvature of the stomach, cut the left gastric网膜血管.
4. Clamp and cut the short gastric vessels one by one, separate the gastric body along the greater curvature of the gastric fundus, free the left side of the cardia, protect the spleen; 
5. Cut the lesser omentum below the liver, free the hepatoduodenal ligament, carefully dissect and separate the enlarged lymph nodes from the space between the left gastric artery, cut and ligate the root, and suture the left gastric artery; separate upwards along the lesser omentum, free the lower segment of the esophagus to the left, clamp and cut the vagus nerve, cut the esophagus 2 cm above the cardia, and place the head of the 25.5 number tube-shaped anastomosis instrument for anastomosis.
6. At the middle and lower third of the gastric body, 5 cm above the upper margin of the mass, clamp the stomach with a 90mm clamp, remove the specimen. Cut the anterior wall of the residual stomach, place the tube-shaped anastomosis instrument for the gastroesophageal end-to-side anastomosis, suture the serosal layer of the anastomosis and the residual stomach to reinforce.
7. Stop bleeding, flush the abdominal cavity, remove the effusion, place two negative pressure balls above and below the anastomosis, count instruments and gauze correctly, layer-by-layer close the abdomen, end of operation.
