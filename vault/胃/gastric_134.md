## Overview
Gastric cancer resection with Billroth I anastomosis, involving the stomach, duodenum, and spleen. Key technical feature includes lymph node dissection and anastomosis technique.

## Diagnosis
**Pre-operative:** Gastric cancer
**Intraoperative:** Gastric cancer confirmed
**Pathological:** Not documented

## Intraoperative Findings
Gastric body posterior wall superficial ulcer near the greater curvature, 0.4cm in diameter, without peritoneum involvement. Lymph node enlargement in the 8th group. Multiple small cysts on the liver surface, 0.3cm in diameter. No significant abnormalities found in other abdominal organs. No ascites.

## Operative Steps
1. General anesthesia, routine disinfection and draping.
2. Midline upper abdominal incision.
3. Dissection of the gastrocolic ligament, right to the second segment of the duodenum, left to the upper 2-3 branches of the short gastric vessels, and ligation.
4. Separation of the anterior leaf to the superior margin of the pancreas.
5. Dissection of the lesser omentum below the liver, ligation of the right gastric and right gastroepiploic vessels at the vascular root, and transection of the duodenum 3cm distal to the pylorus with a purse-string suture for the吻合器 head.
6. Dissection of the left gastric vessels, removal of the gastric coronary vessels and lymphoid tissue on the lesser curvature until below the cardia.
7. Lymph node dissection in the 8th group.
8. Transection of the stomach 4cm above the tumor and removal of the specimen.
9. Opening the anterior wall of the residual stomach, inserting the long tube of the anastomosis device, and performing a side-to-side anastomosis between the posterior wall of the gastric body and the duodenum.
10. Removal of the diseased side of the residual stomach with the anastomosis device, reinforcement of the anastomosis with serosal layer.
11. Hemostasis, lavage of the abdominal cavity, aspiration of exudate, placement of negative pressure balls at the anastomosis site, and suture closure in layers.

## Technical Challenges
Difficulties encountered in lymph node dissection and anastomosis technique.

## Teaching Points
- Importance of lymph node dissection in staging and prognosis.
- Technique of side-to-side anastomosis and reinforcement with serosal layer.

## Complication Prevention
- Proper hemostasis and lavage to prevent infection.
- Careful handling of the anastomotic site to prevent leakage.

断胃结肠韧带之肠侧，右至十二指肠第二段，左至结肠脾曲上2~3支胃短血管并予离断结扎，分离前叶至胰腺上缘。

肝下断小网膜，血管根部断、双重结扎胃右及胃网膜右血管；距幽门远端3cm处断十二指肠，残端作荷包后置入28#吻合器头并固定待用。

向上翻起胃体，血管根部断、双重结扎胃左血管；清除胃冠状血管及小弯侧淋巴组织直至贲门下；清扫第8组淋巴结；

肿瘤上方4cm处断胃，移出标本；

打开残胃前壁，置入吻合器长管，胃体中部后壁靠胃大弯侧与十二指肠行侧端吻合，侧侧吻合器切除病变侧残胃，移去标本，吻合口再浆肌层加固一层；

彻底止血，冲洗腹腔，吸尽渗液，于吻合口处置负压球二根从右上腹戳创引出，逐层关闭。术毕 。
