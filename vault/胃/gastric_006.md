## Overview
Gastrectomy with pyloroplasty for gastric cancer, involving resection of the upper part of the stomach and reconstruction of the pylorus.

## Diagnosis
**Pre-operative:** Gastric cancer
**Intraoperative:** Gastric cancer
**Pathological:** Not documented

## Intraoperative Findings
A 4*3cm ulcerative mass at the cardia, hard, involving the serosa. Lymph nodes NO.1, 2, and 3 palpable. Liver, gallbladder, pancreas, colon, and pelvis unremarkable.

## Operative Steps
1. Supine position, general anesthesia, routine disinfection and draping. Abdominal exploration via a midline upper abdominal incision.
2. Dissection and transection of the gastrocolic ligament along the transverse colon margin, right to the hepatic flexure, preserving the right gastroepiploic vascular arcade. Left transection of the left gastroepiploic vessels, transection of the spleno-gastrointestinal vessels to the left of the cardia, and dissection of NO.2 lymph nodes.
3. Transection of the hepatogastric ligament below the liver, elevation of the gastric body, transection and double ligature of the left gastric vessels at the root, dissection to the right of the cardia, and dissection of NO.1, 3, and 7 lymph nodes. Incision of the esophageal hiatus and bilateral peritoneum, mobilization of the lower esophagus, clamping and transection of the vagus nerve, transection of the esophagus 3cm above the cardia with a purse-string clamp, and insertion of a 25.5# anastomosis钉.
4. Transection of the stomach 6cm below the tumor margin, removal of the specimen, anastomosis of the residual stomach to the esophagus.
5. Transverse transection of the pyloric sphincter, and anastomosis.
6. Thorough hemostasis, irrigation of the abdominal cavity, aspiration of effluent, placement of two negative pressure balls around the esophageal anastomosis, verification of instruments and sponges, and layer-by-layer closure of the abdomen.

## Anatomical Key Points
> Critical structures: esophagus, stomach, pylorus. (食管，胃，幽门)

## Technical Challenges
Not documented

## Teaching Points
- Importance of careful dissection around the cardia and esophagus.
- Proper handling of the left gastric vessels and spleno-gastrointestinal vessels.
- Technique of esophageal and gastric anastomosis.

## Complication Prevention
- Careful hemostasis to prevent postoperative hemorrhage.
- Adequate irrigation to reduce the risk of infection.

## Operative Steps

平卧位气静麻醉成功后，常规消毒铺巾，取上腹正中切口进腹探查如上；

沿横结肠缘分离切断胃结肠韧带，向右至结肠肝曲，保留胃网膜右血管弓，向左切断胃网膜左血管，切断脾胃血管，至贲门左区，清扫NO.2淋巴结。

肝下断肝胃韧带，向上翻起胃体，胃左血管根部切断、双重结扎，向上至贲门右区，清扫NO.1,3,7淋巴结，向左剪开食管裂孔及两侧腹膜，游离食管下段，钳夹、切断迷走神经，距贲门上3cm处荷包钳切断食管，置入25.5#吻合口钉头；

距肿瘤下缘下6cm断胃，移去标本，残胃与食管吻合。

纵行切断幽门括约肌，横行吻合；

彻底止血，冲洗腹腔，吸尽渗液，于食道吻合口四周置负压球两根，清点器械纱布无误，逐层关腹。

术毕。
