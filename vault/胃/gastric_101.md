## Overview
Gastrectomy for gastric cancer, pre-operative diagnosis of gastric cancer, key technical feature: radical resection of the upper part of the stomach.

## Diagnosis
**Pre-operative:** Gastric cancer
**Intraoperative:** Gastric cancer
**Pathological:** Not documented

## Intraoperative Findings
Tumor located on the greater curvature of the stomach at the esophagogastric junction, about 2cm in diameter, elevated ulcerative type.

## Operative Steps
1. Supine position, successful general anesthesia, routine disinfection and draping, midupper abdominal midline incision, layer-by-layer abdominal exploration.
2. Raise the transverse colon, separate and cut the gastrocolic ligament, to the right to the hepatic flexure of the colon, to the left to the inferior pole of the spleen, cut the splenocolic ligament, strip the anterior leaf of the transverse mesocolon to the superior margin of the pancreas, expose the posterior wall of the stomach.
3. Retain the right gastroepiploic vessels, and cut the greater curvature of the stomach at the avascular area of the gastric body, along the greater curvature of the stomach, cut the left gastroepiploic vessels. Open the gastro splenic ligament, clamp and cut the short gastric vessels one by one, separate the gastric body along the greater curvature of the stomach, dissect the second and fourth group of lymph nodes. Free up to the left side of the cardia, protect the spleen.
4. Open the lesser omentum, free the hepatoduodenal ligament, clamp and cut the branches of the coronary vessels along the lesser curvature of the stomach, flip the gastric body open the gastropancreatic fold, ligate and cut the left gastric artery at the root. Separate upwards along the lesser omentum to the cardia, clamp and cut the vagus nerve, free the esophagus upwards about 6cm, clamp and cut with a purse-string clamp 3cm above the tumor margin. The distal gastric stump is placed into a 25.5# tube-shaped anastomosis stapler钉头. The middle segment of the gastric body is cut with a 90mm clamp stapler, remove the specimen. The distal gastric stump is reinforced with silk thread.
5. Open the anterior wall of the distal gastric body, insert the long handle of the 25.5# tube-shaped anastomosis stapler, anastomose with the esophagus at the posterior wall. The anastomosis is reinforced with 3-0 absorbable suture. Close the window.
6. Stop bleeding, clean the abdominal cavity, place two negative pressure drainage tubes at the upper and lower ends of the anastomosis and lead out through abdominal wall puncture wounds, count sponges and instruments, close the abdomen, end of operation.

## Anatomical Key Points
> Critical structures: stomach, esophagus, spleen, liver, colon. (胆总管)

## Teaching Points
- The importance of protecting the spleen during dissection.
- The technique of anastomosis with a tube-shaped stapler.
- The management of lymph node dissection.

## Complication Prevention
- Adequate hemostasis.
- Proper anastomosis technique to prevent leaks.
