## Overview
Gastric cancer resection with D2 lymph node dissection and Billroth I reconstruction, performed for gastric cancer.

## Diagnosis
**Pre-operative:** Gastric cancer
**Intraoperative:** Gastric cancer confirmed
**Pathological:** Not documented

## Intraoperative Findings
Gastric antrum mass measuring 4*3cm, infiltrative ulcerative type, circumferential around the gastric antrum, invasion to serosal layer, palpable enlarged lymph nodes in 7th, 8th, and 11th groups. No abnormalities found in liver, gallbladder, pancreas, spleen, small intestine, colon, and peritoneal cavity.

## Operative Steps
1. General anesthesia, supine position, routine disinfection and draping, midline upper abdominal incision.
2. Dissection of the gastrocolic ligament, right to the second segment of the duodenum, left to the upper 2-3 branches of the left gastric short vessels, and ligation.
3. Separation of the anterior leaf to the superior margin of the pancreas.
4. Dissection of the subhepatic omentum, stripping of the anterior leaf of the hepatoduodenal ligament and its lymphatic tissue.
5. Dissection and double ligation of the right gastric and greater omentum vessels at the vascular root, and lymph node dissection of the 6th group.
6. Dissection along the subhepatic part of the gastrohepatic ligament, dissection of No.12a lymph node along the hepatic artery, dissection of the 5th group lymph nodes, and up to the common hepatic artery, dissection of the 8th group lymph nodes.
7. Transection of the stomach 8cm above the tumor, removal of specimen.
8. Transection of the duodenum 3cm distal to the pylorus, placement of the 28# anastomosis stapler head.
9. Opening the anterior wall of the residual stomach, insertion of the stapler long tube, side-to-side anastomosis of the middle posterior wall of the gastric body with the duodenum, side-to-side stapler resection of the diseased side of the residual stomach, removal of specimen, and reinforcement of the anastomosis with a layer of serosal muscle.
10. Thorough hemostasis, lavage of the peritoneal cavity, aspiration of effluent fluid, placement of two negative pressure balls at the anastomosis, and layer-by-layer closure.

## Intraoperative Findings
术中见胃窦部肿块4*3cm，浸润溃疡型，绕胃窦部一周，侵犯至浆膜层，第7、8、11组可及肿大淋巴结。探查肝、胆、胰、脾、小肠、结肠、腹盆腔无殊。

## Operative Steps

气静麻醉后平卧位，常规消毒铺巾，取上腹正中切口进腹，所见如上。

断胃结肠韧带之肠侧，右至十二指肠第二段，左至结肠脾曲上2~3支胃短血管并予离断结扎，分离前叶至胰腺上缘。

肝下断小网膜，剥离肝十二指肠韧带前叶及其中之淋巴组织。

血管根部断、双重结扎胃右及胃网膜右血管，清扫第6组淋巴结

沿肝下切断肝胃韧带，沿肝动脉清扫No.12a淋巴结，根部切断胃右血管，清扫第5组淋巴结，并至肝总动脉，清扫第8组淋巴结.

肿瘤上方8cm处断胃，移出标本；

距幽门远端3cm处断十二指肠，残端作荷包后置入28#吻合器头。

打开残胃前壁，置入吻合器长管，胃体中部后壁靠胃大弯侧与十二指肠行侧端吻合，侧侧吻合器切除病变侧残胃，移去标本，吻合口再浆肌层加固一层；

彻底止血，冲洗腹腔，吸尽渗液，于吻合口处置负压球二根从右上腹戳创引出，逐层关闭。术毕 。
