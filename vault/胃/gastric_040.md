## Overview
Gastric cancer resection with Billroth I reconstruction and D2 lymph node dissection. Involves extensive dissection and lymphadenectomy around the stomach and adjacent structures.

## Diagnosis
**Pre-operative:** Gastric cancer
**Intraoperative:** Gastric cancer confirmed
**Pathological:** Not documented

## Intraoperative Findings
Tumor located on the lesser curvature near the gastric angle, approximately 5cm in diameter, infiltrative ulcerative type, extending beyond the serosa. Lymph nodes in groups 1, 2, 3, 7, and 8 were enlarged. No liver or pelvic involvement.

## Operative Steps
1. General anesthesia, routine disinfection and draping. Abdominal exploration via an upper midline incision.
2. Dissection of the gastrocolic ligament, right to the ampulla of the duodenum below, left to the splenic flexure superiorly, and transection and ligation of 2-3 branches of the left gastric short vessels.
3. Dissection below the pylorus to expose the right gastric网膜血管, transection and ligation at the root, and lymphadenectomy of group 6.
4. Dissection below the liver to transect the lesser omentum, stripping the anterior leaf of the hepatoduodenal ligament and lymphoid tissue, and exposing the right gastric vessel, proper hepatic artery, and celiac trunk. Transection and ligation at the root of the right gastric vessel, and lymphadenectomy of groups 7, 8, and 12.
5. Transection of the duodenum 3cm distal to the pylorus, placing the 28.5mm anvil of the anastomotic stapler and securing it. Raising the gastric body, ligating and suture-ligating the root of the left gastric vessel; removing the specimen, and placing the long tube of the stapler into the opening of the remaining stomach.
6. Anastomosis of the gastric body to the duodenal stump at the middle third of the gastric body, posterior wall of the greater curvature, and the duodenal stump. Suturing the serosal layer of the anastomosis with interrupted sutures and full-thickness with interrupted sutures.
7. Thorough hemostasis, irrigation of the abdominal cavity, aspiration of effluent, and placement of two negative-pressure drains at the anastomotic site.
8. Counting sponges and instruments, closing the abdomen in layers, and completion of the procedure.

## Anatomical Key Points
> Critical structures: gastric antrum, gastrocolic ligament, omentum, common bile duct (胆总管), gallbladder, pancreas, spleen, duodenum, colon, ileum, mesentery, lymph nodes. (胆总管)

## Intraoperative Findings
术中见肿瘤位于小弯侧近胃角，直径约5cm，浸润溃疡型，侵及浆膜外。另见第1、2、3、7、8组淋巴结肿大，肝、盆腔（-）。

## Operative Steps

气静麻醉后常规消毒铺巾，取上腹正中切口进腹，探查如上；

断胃结肠韧带之肠侧，右至十二指肠球部下方，左至结肠脾曲上2~3支胃短血管并予离断结扎，剥离横结肠系膜前叶至胰腺上缘；

幽门下分离显露胃网膜右血管，在其根部双重切断之，并清扫第6组淋巴结；

肝下离断小网膜，向右剥离肝十二指肠韧带前叶及其中之淋巴组织，并显露胃右血管、肝固有动脉、腹腔干，在胃右血管根部处切断并双重结扎，清扫第7、8、12组淋巴结。

距幽门远端3cm处断十二指肠，残端作荷包后置入28.5mm吻合器钉砧并固定，向上翻起胃体，血管根部结扎+缝扎并离断胃左血管；清除胃冠状血管及小弯侧淋巴组织（第1，3组）直至贲门下；

距肿块上缘约5cm处两把考克钳钳闭胃体并开口，余胃体90mm钳闭器钳闭切断，移去标本，残胃开口内置入吻合器长管，胃体中近三分之一处大弯侧后壁与十二指肠残端吻合，吻合口浆肌层间断加固一周，全层间断缝合胃体开口。

彻底止血，冲洗腹腔，吸尽渗液，于吻合口前后置负压引流两根。

清点纱布器械无误，逐层关腹，术毕。
