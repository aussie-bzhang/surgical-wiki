## Overview
PPH for hemorrhoids; pre-operative diagnosis of hemorrhoids; key technical feature of rectal mucosal resection using PPH device.

## Diagnosis
**Pre-operative:** Hemorrhoids
**Intraoperative:** Hemorrhoids
**Pathological:** Not documented

## Intraoperative Findings
Anal orifice with prolapsed hemorrhoids; prominent hemorrhoids above and below the dentate line, most prominent at 7 o'clock position.

## Operative Steps
1. Post-anesthesia saddle position, routine disinfection and draping;
2. After dilation, perform double purse-string suture 3-4 cm above the dentate line;
3. Insert 31-size PPH tube, resect approximately 3 cm of rectal mucosa;
4. Perfect hemostasis, insert gauze soaked in Vaseline into the anal canal for hemostasis, end of surgery.

## Anatomical Key Points
> Rectum, anal canal (肛管); common bile duct (胆总管) (if applicable)

## Technical Challenges
Not documented

## Teaching Points
- Proper placement and tensioning of the PPH suture line
- Adequate hemostasis during and after resection

## Complication Prevention
- Proper hemostasis to prevent postoperative hemorrhage
