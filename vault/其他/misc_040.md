## Overview
Splenectomy with cyst decortication and drainage for a splenic cyst, a common clinical scenario requiring surgical intervention.

## Diagnosis
**Pre-operative:** Splenic cyst
**Intraoperative:** Splenic cyst confirmed
**Pathological:** Not documented

## Intraoperative Findings
A 8.0 cm cystic mass superior to the spleen with pale yellow cyst fluid.

## Operative Steps
1. Supine position, under general anesthesia, routine disinfection and draping.
2. Three-port laparoscopic approach, insufflation, positioning, and intraoperative findings as described.
3. Ultrasonic knife resection of part of the cyst wall, approximately 4*4 cm in size, and drainage of cyst fluid.
4. Irrigation, hemostasis, and instrument count confirmed. One negative pressure tube was placed for drainage. The incisions were closed sequentially, and the procedure was completed.

## Anatomical Key Points
> Spleen (脾脏), cyst (囊肿). Critical structures include the splenic hilum and surrounding vasculature. (脾门及周围血管)

## Technical Challenges
Not documented

## Teaching Points
- The importance of accurate cyst localization and resection.
- The technique of cyst drainage and negative pressure tube placement.

## Complication Prevention
- Proper hemostasis to prevent postoperative hemorrhage.
- Careful dissection to avoid injury to adjacent structures.
