## Overview
Splenectomy with perigastric vascular ligation for liver cirrhosis, portal hypertension, and splenic hyperactivity. Key technical feature includes selective splenic artery ligation and perigastric vascular disconnection.

## Diagnosis
**Pre-operative:** Liver cirrhosis, portal hypertension, splenic hyperactivity
**Intraoperative:** Liver cirrhosis, portal hypertension, splenic hyperactivity
**Pathological:** Not documented

## Intraoperative Findings
Spleen measuring 25*20*12cm, marked varices at the fundus and lower esophagus, liver showing mixed nodular cirrhosis, no ascites. Intraoperative pressure: 26mmHg at entry, 23mmHg after splenic artery ligation, 26mmHg after splenectomy, 22mmHg after disconnection.

## Operative Steps
1. Supine position, under general anesthesia, routine disinfection and draping. Left upper quadrant 'L' incision for abdominal access.
2. Incise the gastrocolic ligament, divide the splenogastric ligament up to the left of the cardia, find and ligate the splenic artery above the pancreas.
3. Divide the splenocolic, splenorenal, and splenodiaphragmatic ligaments, and fully mobilize the splenic pedicle.
4. Separate the splenic artery and vein, ligate and tie them separately, remove the spleen, ligate and tie the gastric pancreas and left subdiaphragmatic vessels.
5. Divide and ligate the gastric coronary vessels along the lesser curvature of the stomach, mobilize up to the right of the cardia.
6. Mobilize the lower esophagus, divide the vagus nerve, and proceed up to the esophageal hiatus, divide or ligate the esophageal varices.
7. Rinse the abdominal cavity with normal saline, achieve hemostasis, place two negative pressure drains below the left diaphragm and behind the esophagus.
8. Count sponges and instruments, close the abdomen in layers, surgery completed.

## Anatomical Key Points
> Spleen, gastric fundus, esophagus, portal vein, splenic artery. (脾脏, 胃底, 食管, 门静脉, 脾动脉)

Not documented

## Teaching Points
- Selective splenic artery ligation is critical for minimizing blood loss.
- Proper mobilization of the spleen and its pedicle is essential to avoid injury to adjacent structures.
- Disconnection of the perigastric vascular arcade is a key step in preventing variceal bleeding.

## Complication Prevention
- Adequate hemostasis is crucial to prevent postoperative hemorrhage.
- Proper placement of drains helps in preventing postoperative ascites and infection.

## Operative Steps

取平卧位，气静麻下常规消毒铺巾，取左上腹‘L’型切口进腹，探查如上；

打开胃结肠韧带，向上断脾胃韧带直至贲门左区，于胰腺上方胰尾部寻找到脾动脉后予结扎；

断脾结肠韧带、脾肾韧带和脾膈韧带，充分游离脾蒂；

分离脾动静脉，分别切断后双重结扎，移去脾脏，断扎胃胰间血管和左膈下血管；

沿胃小弯断扎胃冠状血管，向上游离至贲门右区；

游离食管下端，切断迷走神经，向上至食管裂孔，切断或缝扎食管曲张静脉；

大量生理盐水清洗腹腔，完善止血，于左膈下、食管后置负压引流二根；

清点纱布器械无误，逐层关腹，术闭。
