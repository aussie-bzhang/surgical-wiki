## Overview
Excision of a cervical lymph node for a neck mass in a patient with a clinical presentation of a neck mass.

## Diagnosis
**Pre-operative:** Neck mass
**Intraoperative:** Neck mass
**Pathological:** Not documented

## Intraoperative Findings
Intraoperative findings revealed a neck mass located near the right posterior neck and close to the mastoid process, measuring approximately 1.5 cm in diameter, with soft consistency.

## Operative Steps
1. Supine position, under general anesthesia, routine disinfection and draping.
2. Longitudinal incision of the skin, layer-by-layer dissection, exploration as described above.
3. Careful separation of the mass from surrounding tissues, complete excision of the mass, and removal of the specimen.
4. Thorough hemostasis, layer-by-layer closure of the incision.
5. Operation completed.
