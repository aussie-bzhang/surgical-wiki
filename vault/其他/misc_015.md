## Overview
Splenectomy with gastrovascular ligation and cholecystectomy for liver cirrhosis, portal hypertension, splenic hyperactivity, and cholecystolithiasis. Key technical feature includes ligation of gastrovascular structures and gallbladder resection.

## Diagnosis
**Pre-operative:** Liver cirrhosis, portal hypertension, splenic hyperactivity, cholecystolithiasis
**Intraoperative:** Liver cirrhosis, portal hypertension, splenic hyperactivity, cholecystolithiasis
**Pathological:** Not documented

## Intraoperative Findings
Spleen 15*10*10cm, congestive, no significant varices at the lower esophageal gastric junction. Spleenic artery diameter 1cm, splenic vein diameter 1.5cm. Liver shows mixed nodular cirrhosis, right lobe atrophy severe. Gallbladder bed sunken, posterior displacement, gallbladder size 10*3*2cm, containing a single mixed stone, diameter 3.5cm. Gallbladder duct diameter 0.5cm, CBD diameter approximately 0.8cm.

## Operative Steps
1. Supine position, under general anesthesia, routine disinfection and draping. Midline upper abdominal incision around the umbilicus, abdominal exploration as above.
2. Transect splenic colonic, renal, and diaphragmatic ligaments, fully mobilize the splenic pedicle.
3. Separate splenic artery and vein, ligate and tie separately, remove spleen. Transect and ligate gastropancreatic and left subdiaphragmatic vessels. Suture ligate the coronary vessels along the lesser curvature of the stomach.
4. Carefully dissect the gallbladder triangle, mobilize the cystic duct and gallbladder vessels. Ligate and suture the cystic duct 0.5cm from the CBD, ligate the cystic vessels. Strip the gallbladder from the liver gallbladder bed and remove the specimen.
5. Rinse the abdominal cavity with large amounts of normal saline, perfect hemostasis. Place two negative pressure drains in the splenic fossa and gallbladder bed under the liver.
6. Count sponges and instruments, close the abdomen in layers, surgery completed.

## Anatomical Key Points
> Spleen, gastrovascular structures, gallbladder. Common bile duct (胆总管).
