## Overview
Pancreatic body tumor resection with splenic sparing, a procedure performed for the removal of a pancreatic body tumor while preserving the spleen.

## Diagnosis
**Pre-operative:** Pancreatic body tumor
**Intraoperative:** Pancreatic body tumor
**Pathological:** Not documented

## Intraoperative Findings
Intraoperative findings revealed a 2.5cm hard mass in the pancreatic body, closely adherent to the splenic artery and vein. No significant abnormalities were observed in the liver, stomach, and small intestine during exploration.

## Operative Steps
1. Supine position, under general anesthesia, routine disinfection and draping. Abdominal exploration via an upper midline incision.
2. Dissection of the gastrocolic ligament, opening of the omental bursa, and exposure of the pancreas. Resection of the pancreatic body tail 2.0cm from the mass, with ligation and binding of the pancreatic stump.
3. Dissection and transection of the splenic renal ligament and splenic diaphragmatic ligament, freeing and protecting the splenic artery and vein to the splenic pedicle, and removal of the specimen.
4. Hemostasis, suture of the posterior peritoneum, placement of two negative pressure drains at the pancreatic stump and splenic fossa, puncture and extraction, and confirmation of instruments and sponges. Layered closure of the abdomen.

## Anatomical Key Points
> Critical structures: pancreatic body, spleen, splenic artery and vein. (胆总管)

Not documented

## Teaching Points
- Importance of preserving the splenic artery and vein during dissection.
- Proper hemostasis and closure techniques to prevent postoperative complications.

## Complication Prevention
- Proper hemostasis to prevent postoperative hemorrhage.
- Adequate drainage to prevent postoperative abscess formation.

## Intraoperative Findings
术中见胰体直径2.5cm肿块，质硬，与脾动静脉致密粘连，探查肝胃小肠等脏器未见明显异常。

## Operative Steps

取平卧位，气静麻下常规消毒铺巾，取上腹正中切口进腹，探查如上；

分离胃结肠韧带，打开网膜囊，显露胰腺。距肿块2.0cm处切除胰体尾，胰腺残端缝扎加捆绑；

分离切断脾肾韧带、脾膈韧带，游离并保护脾动静脉至脾蒂，移除标本；

完善止血，缝合后腹膜，胰腺残端及脾窝置负压两根，戳孔引出，清点器械纱布无误，逐层关腹。

术毕。
