## Overview
Pancreaticoduodenectomy with Child reconstruction for obstructive jaundice due to distal bile duct cancer following rectal and cholecystectomy. Key technical feature includes complex adhesiolysis and reconstruction of the biliary-enteric system.

## Diagnosis
**Pre-operative:** Obstructive jaundice, distal bile duct cancer following rectal and cholecystectomy.
**Intraoperative:** Confirmed by visualization of a hard mass at the distal bile duct near the head of the pancreas.
**Pathological:** Not documented

## Intraoperative Findings
Liver showed cholestatic changes, bile duct was significantly dilated to 2.0 cm in diameter. A hard mass of 2*3 cm was palpable near the head of the pancreas, causing complete obstruction of the bile duct with a large amount of white bile. Severe intra-abdominal adhesions, significant edema of stomach, small intestine, and bile duct. Negative exploration of liver, small intestine, colon, pelvis, and abdominal wall. Intraoperative blood loss of approximately 1000 ml, transfusion of RBC 1000 ml and plasma 600 ml.

## Operative Steps
1. Patient placed in supine position after successful general anesthesia. Routine disinfection and draping. Upper midline incision, excision of previous scar, and entry into the abdomen.
2. Careful dissection of intra-abdominal adhesions to avoid injury to organs and intestines.
3. Dissection of the greater omentum and mobilization of the left side to the splenic flexure and the right side to the hepatogastric region. Ligature and transection of the right gastric and greater curvature vessels, preserving the left gastric vessels.
4. Mobilization of the posterior wall of the stomach along the pancreatic capsule.
5. Dissection of the hepatoduodenal ligament, mobilization of the common bile duct, hepatic artery, and portal vein. Lymph node dissection of the 12th group. Mobilization and identification of the celiac trunk, common hepatic artery, left gastric artery, and splenic artery. Double ligature and transection of the gastroduodenal artery.
6. CBD transection at the confluence of the right and left hepatic ducts for decompression, with hemostasis at the proximal断面.
7. Kocher incision, mobilization of the descending duodenum, and identification of the distal bile duct tumor. Ligature and transection of the right gastric and lesser curvature vessels, and proximal suture and ligature of the left gastric vessels.
8. Mobilization of the duodenum and pancreas, transection of the duodenopancreatic ligament, and mobilization of the duodenum 2-3 segments.
9. Transection of the jejunojejunal ligament and transection of the jejunal segment, complete resection of the pancreatoduodenal specimen.
10. Mobilization of the jejunal segment and jejunojejunal anastomosis.
11. Completion of the biliary-enteric anastomosis.
12. Reconstruction of the gastrointestinal tract using the Child method.
13. Hemostasis and placement of drains.

## Technical Challenges
Difficulties encountered included severe adhesions, significant edema, and the need for meticulous hemostasis.

## Teaching Points
- Importance of careful dissection and mobilization to avoid injury to adjacent structures.
- The need for precise hemostasis during the resection and anastomosis.
- The technique of Child reconstruction for biliary-enteric reconstruction.

## Complication Prevention
- Careful dissection to avoid injury to adjacent structures.
- Meticulous hemostasis to prevent postoperative hemorrhage.
- Adequate decompression of the bile duct to prevent postoperative jaundice.

## Intraoperative Findings
术中见肝脏呈淤胆性，胆总管明显扩张至直径约2.0cm，胆总管下段近胰头处可及一质硬肿块，大小约2*3cm，致使胆总管完全阻塞，内含大量白胆汁，腹腔内粘连较严重，胃、小肠及胆管水肿较明显，探查肝脏、小肠、结肠、盆腔及腹壁均阴性，术中出血约1000ml，术中输血RBC1000ml、血浆600ml。

## Operative Steps

气静麻成功后取平卧位，常规消毒铺巾，取上腹正中切口、切除原手术疤痕逐层进腹，仔细分解腹腔内粘连避免损伤脏器肠管，探查具体如上述。

切除横结肠系膜前叶并游离切断胃结肠韧带左侧至横结肠脾曲、右侧至结肠肝区，结扎切断胃网膜右血管及大弯侧血管并保留胃短血管，沿胰腺包膜游离胃后壁。

解剖肝十二指肠韧带，游离胆总管、肝动脉及门静脉，清扫第十二组淋巴结，游离解剖腹腔干，明确肝总动脉、胃左动脉及脾动脉，游离胃十二指肠动脉双道结扎切断，左右肝管汇合部切断CBD行减压，近端断面止血待吻合。

行kocher切口，游离十二指肠降部外侧腹膜及疏松结缔组织，向左侧翻起可扪及胆总管下段肿瘤，结扎切断胃右血管及小弯侧血管，近根部缝扎+结扎胃左血管，可将胃体向上翻起，切断胃胰襞游离至胰腺上缘，保留约1/3胃体以55mm侧侧吻合器断之待吻合。

于胰腺下缘游离肠系膜上静脉，沿SMV前壁小心游离胰腺，于胰腺上下缘各缝扎一道并作牵引，沿SMV前壁断胰腺组织，断面电凝止血。

仔细分离切断结扎胰腺钩突与肠系膜上血管的分支并切断胰腺钩突（其中小血管以1-0缝线小心双道结扎后切断），游离十二指肠二三段，游离屈氏韧带下方约20cm空肠系膜并断肠段，完整切除胰十二指肠标本。

将空肠断段自结肠系膜中部无血管区牵引至结肠上区，游离胰腺断段约3.0cm（注意小心结扎胰腺与脾静脉之间细小血管避免出血），完成胰肠捆绑式吻合。

距离胰肠吻合口约15cm处空肠对系膜缘打开肠壁，直径略大于CBD直径，间断全层外翻缝合完成胆肠端侧吻合。

完善止血，沿肝下缘经胆肠吻合口于胰肠吻合口处留置负压引流两根，自右上腹引出。
