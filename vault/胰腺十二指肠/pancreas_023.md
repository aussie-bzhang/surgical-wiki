## Overview
Pancreatic tail resection and splenectomy for residual empyema in the abdominal cavity, with significant portal hypertension and splenic congestion.

## Diagnosis
**Pre-operative:** Retroperitoneal cyst
**Intraoperative:** Residual empyema in the body and tail of the pancreas
**Pathological:** Not documented

## Intraoperative Findings
Large cystic mass in the body and tail of the pancreas, 10*8*6cm in size, containing a large amount of old blood. The mass extends downward along the left paracolic沟 to the anterior surface of the left kidney. Regional portal hypertension in the abdominal cavity, with marked varices of the left gastric vein and splenic congestion.

## Operative Steps
1. Supine position, under general anesthesia, routine disinfection and draping, midline upper abdominal incision.
2. Dissection of the gastrocolic ligament, opening of the omental bursa, exposure of the pancreas.
3. Dissection of the hepatoduodenal ligament, transection of the common hepatic artery, mobilization of the superior margin of the pancreas and portal vein.
4. Transection and ligation of the splenogastric ligament superiorly, separation and transection of the splenocolic ligament inferiorly. Mobilization of the superior mesenteric vein and splenic vein at the root of the mesentery, transection of the body of the pancreas 1cm away from the mass, and locking suture ligation of the pancreatic stump.
5. Use of atraumatic clamps to occlude the splenic vein on the left side of the SMV, transection of the splenic vein, secure suture ligation of the proximal end, and ligature of the distal end. Ligation and transection of the splenic artery.
6. Dissection and transection of the splenorenal, splenodiaphragmatic ligaments, and the tail of the pancreas and spleen, removal of the specimen. Dissection of the residual empyema wall along the left paracolic沟 to the anterior surface of the left kidney.
7. Thorough hemostasis, repeated lavage of the abdominal cavity, closure of the posterior peritoneum, application of hemostatic powder, packing with hemostatic gauze. Confirmation of no bleeding. Placement of two negative pressure drains at the pancreatic stump and one at the splenic fossa, puncture and extraction, counting instruments and sponges, layer-by-layer closure of the abdomen.
