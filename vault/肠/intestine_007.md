## Overview
Sigmoid colon resection for sigmoid colon cancer, involving the ileocecal junction and rectum, with emphasis on vascular and lymphatic dissection.

## Diagnosis
**Pre-operative:** Sigmoid colon cancer
**Intraoperative:** Sigmoid colon cancer
**Pathological:** Not documented

## Intraoperative Findings
Intraoperative findings revealed a 5cm hard mass in the distal sigmoid colon, involving the serosa and encircling the bowel for 3/4 of its circumference. A 3cm hard mass was also found 5cm proximal to the primary mass. No findings in the proximal colon, small intestine, liver, or pelvis.

## Operative Steps
1. Position the patient in supine position under general anesthesia, perform routine disinfection and draping.
2. Midline incision below the umbilicus, enter peritoneum layer by layer, and expose the sigmoid colon and pelvic peritoneum.
3. Mobilize the sigmoid colon mesentery, and transect the sigmoid colon vessels at the root.
4. Mobilize the proximal sigmoid colon to 7cm above the mass and place a 28.5mm anastomosis stapler. Protect the ureter and iliac vessels.
5. Mobilize the distal sigmoid colon to 5cm below the mass, transect the distal rectum, remove the specimen, and clean the distal rectum.
6. Perform end-to-end anastomosis with a 28.5mm circular stapler, and suture the serosal layer around the anastomosis.
7. Place two negative-pressure drains in the pelvic floor.
8. Irrigate, ensure hemostasis, count instruments and sponges, and close the incision in layers.

## Anatomical Key Points
> Critical structures include the sigmoid colon, ileocecal junction, rectum, vessels, and lymph nodes. The common bile duct (胆总管) and ureter should be protected during dissection.

## Technical Challenges
Difficulties encountered include extensive adhesions, large tumor size, and protecting adjacent structures.

## Teaching Points
- Proper mobilization and dissection of the sigmoid colon mesentery.
- Identification and protection of the ureter and iliac vessels.
- Accurate placement of the anastomosis stapler.

## Complication Prevention
- Infection prevention through meticulous surgical technique and antibiotic prophylaxis.
- Hemorrhage prevention through careful hemostasis.

## Operative Steps

*取平卧位，气静麻下常规消毒铺巾。

*下腹正中绕脐切口，逐层进腹，术中所见如上。

*将小肠推向上腹，显露乙状结肠及盆底腹膜，打开侧腹膜，游离乙状结肠系膜，于根部切断乙结肠血管。

*切断乙结肠系膜，近端游离至肿块上方7cm，切断后置入28.5mm吻合器抵针，注意输尿管及髂血管走向并保护。远端分离至肿块下方5cm腹膜反折下，

*断远端直肠，移去标本，清洗远端直肠至净后置入28.5mm管状吻合器，行端端吻合，吻合口浆肌层间断缝合一周加固。关闭系膜裂孔。

*置负压引流二根于盆底。

*冲洗，完善止血，清点器械纱布无误，逐层关闭切口。

*术毕。
