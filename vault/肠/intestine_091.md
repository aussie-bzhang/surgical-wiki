## Overview
Rectal cancer resection (Miles procedure) for rectal adenocarcinoma with invasion of the muscularis propria. Key technical feature includes radical resection of the rectum and anal canal.

## Diagnosis
**Pre-operative:** Rectal mass
**Intraoperative:** Rectal adenocarcinoma
**Pathological:** Adenocarcinoma, invasion of the muscularis propria

## Intraoperative Findings
Tumor located at the anal verge, diameter 3.0cm, cauliflower-like, soft, circumferential around the rectum, located at 5-11 o'clock position in the lithotomy position. Exploration of liver and omentum negative.

## Operative Steps
1. Supine position, routine disinfection and draping under general anesthesia.
2. Midline lower abdominal incision, enter peritoneum layer by layer, findings as described above.
3. Push the small intestine upwards, expose the rectum and pelvic peritoneum, open the lateral peritoneum, free the mesosigmoid, expose and protect both ureters, open the peritoneal reflection on the anterior rectal wall, transect the rectal vessels at the root, transect the mesosigmoid and rectal mesentery, transect the lateral ligaments of the rectum,锐性分离骶前间隙直至尾骨尖，充分游离直肠前壁至深部.
4. Suture the anal margin, make an elliptical incision centered on the anus, incise the skin and subcutaneous fat, remove the fat tissue in the rectosacral fossa.
5. Expose and transect the anal tail ligament, transect the levator ani muscles on both sides, pull the rectum downwards, separate and transect the connection between the urogenital diaphragm and the anterior rectal wall, drag the rectal specimen out through the perineal wound, remove the specimen.
6. Irrigation, hemostasis, place two negative pressure drains in the sacral area, lead out through the perineal wound.
7. Reconstruct the pelvic peritoneum, make a circular incision in the left lower abdomen, incise to the peritoneum, drag out the proximal sigmoid colon for colostomy.
8. Count instruments and sponges, close the abdomen in layers, end of operation.
