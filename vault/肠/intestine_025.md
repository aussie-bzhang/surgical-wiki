## Overview
Right hemicolectomy for right colon cancer with adhesion release. Involves resection of the right colon and adjacent structures, followed by adhesion release. Key technical feature includes mesocolon mobilization and colonic anastomosis.

## Diagnosis
**Pre-operative:** Right colon cancer
**Intraoperative:** Right colon cancer confirmed
**Pathological:** Not documented

## Intraoperative Findings
Severe adhesions in the upper right abdomen. Tumor located in the cecum, approximately 5cm in diameter, infiltrative ulcerative type, hard consistency, involving serosa, with palpable enlarged lymph nodes. Exploration of liver, stomach, and pelvis negative.

## Operative Steps
1. Supine position, under general anesthesia, routine disinfection and draping.
2. Midline incision, enter peritoneum layer by layer, findings as described above.
3. Bluntly separate adhesions in the upper right abdomen, mobilize gastrocolic ligament, divide the right greater omentum, mobilize the transverse mesocolon, divide the hepatocolic ligament, separate the colon from the duodenum, mobilize the hepatic flexure of the colon.
4. Open the right paracolic fascia, mobilize the ascending colon mesocolon, pay attention to the course of the ureter, mobilize the ileocecal region and distal ileum.
5. Divide the right middle colic artery, ileocolic artery, and right colic artery, ligate at the root, divide the mesocolon, divide the ileum 20cm from the ileocecal junction, and divide the colon at the middle of the transverse colon to remove the specimen.
6. Place the anvil of the anastomosis stapler in the distal ileum, make an incision on the colonic serosa of the transverse colon, use a 28.5mm circular stapler for ileocolic end-to-side anastomosis, close the colonic stump with a 60mm clamp. Suture the anastomosis and stump with gastrointestinal suture for reinforcement, close the mesenteric defect.
7. Irrigation, hemostasis, count instruments and sponges, place two negative pressure balls in the right paracolic gutter, pelvic cavity, and adjacent to the anastomosis, place them upwards and downwards, close the incision one by one, end of operation.
