## Overview
A colorectal procedure involving the resection of a posterior retroperitoneal tumor and a right hemicolectomy due to a right colon cancer.

## Diagnosis
**Pre-operative:** Right lower quadrant mass
**Intraoperative:** Mesenteric tumor at ileocecal junction
**Pathological:** Not documented

## Intraoperative Findings
Intraoperative findings revealed a mesenteric tumor at the ileocecal junction, measuring 7*4*4cm, with a smooth margin and adhesion to the ureter. The liver, gallbladder, pancreas, spleen, small intestine, and colon were unremarkable.

## Operative Steps
1. Supine position, under general anesthesia, routine disinfection and draping.
2. Midline incision, enter peritoneum layer by layer, findings as described above.
3. Dissection of gastrocolic ligament, transection of the right omentum, mobilization of the transverse colon mesentery, transection of the hepatocolic ligament, separation of the colon from the duodenum, mobilization of the colon hepatic flexure.
4. Incision of the lateral peritoneum of the right side, mobilization of the ascending colon mesentery, attention to the course of the ureter, careful separation of the tumor from the ureteral adhesion, mobilization of the ileocecal junction and terminal ileum.
5. Transection of the right branch of the middle colonic artery, ileocolic artery, and right colonic artery, ligation at the root, transection of the colon mesentery, transection of the ileum 20cm from the ileocecal junction, transection of the colon at the middle of the transverse colon to remove the specimen.
6. Partial invasion of the abdominal wall by the tumor, so excision was performed.
7. 28F tube anastomosis stapler for ileocolic end-to-side anastomosis, clamping the colon stump, closing the mesenteric defect.
8. Irrigation, hemostasis, count instruments and sponges, place two negative pressure drainage tubes in the right colon paracolic沟 pelvis, close the incision sequentially, end of surgery.

## Anatomical Key Points
> Critical structures: ileocecal junction, right colon, ureter. (回盲部，右结肠，输尿管)

## Teaching Points
- Importance of careful mobilization around the ileocecal junction and ureter.
- Proper handling of the colon mesentery to avoid injury to blood vessels and nerves.
- Technique for using the stapler for ileocolic anastomosis.

## Complication Prevention
- Careful hemostasis to prevent postoperative hemorrhage.
- Adequate irrigation to reduce the risk of infection.
