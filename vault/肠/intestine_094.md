## Overview
Right hemicolectomy for cecum cancer, involving resection of the ascending colon and terminal ileum, with anastomosis of the ileum to the transverse colon.

## Diagnosis
**Pre-operative:** Cecal cancer
**Intraoperative:** Cecal cancer
**Pathological:** Not documented

## Intraoperative Findings
Tumor located in the middle segment of the ascending colon, infiltrative ulcerative type, diameter approximately 4.0 cm, circumferential around the bowel wall, tumor突破至浆膜外. No metastasis to liver, stomach, small intestine, or pelvis. No ascites.

## Operative Steps
1. Supine position, under general anesthesia, routine disinfection and draping.
2. Upper midline incision, enter peritoneum layer by layer, findings as described.
3. Dissection of gastrocolic ligament, transection of right omentum, mobilization of transverse mesocolon, transection of hepatocolic ligament, separation of colon from duodenum, mobilization of the hepatic flexure of the colon.
4. Transection of right branch of middle colonic vessels, ileocolic vessels, and right colic vessels, ligation at the root, and lymph node dissection.
5. Incision of the lateral peritoneum of the right side, dissection of adhesions, mobilization of the mesentery of the ascending colon, attention to the course of the ureter, mobilization of the ileocecal junction and terminal ileum.
6. Transection of mesocolon, transection of ileum 20 cm from the ileocecal junction, transection of colon at the middle of the transverse colon, removal of specimen.
7. Anastomosis of ileum to transverse colon using a 28.5 mm circular stapler, suture reinforcement of the anastomosis, closure of the distal stump with a clamp, external reinforcement suture, closure of the mesenteric defect.
8. Irrigation, hemostasis, count instruments and sponges, placement of two negative pressure drainage balls in the right paracolic沟 of the pelvis, closure of the incision, completion of the operation.

## Anatomical Key Points
> Critical structures: ascending colon, ileocecal junction, mesentery, vessels, lymph nodes. (升结肠，回盲部，系膜，血管，淋巴结)

## Technical Challenges
Difficulties encountered in mobilization of the hepatic flexure of the colon and lymph node dissection.

## Teaching Points
- Importance of identifying and protecting the ureter during mobilization.
- Proper technique for lymph node dissection.
- Correct use of stapler for anastomosis.

## Complication Prevention
- Adequate hemostasis.
- Proper anastomotic technique to prevent anastomotic leakage.
